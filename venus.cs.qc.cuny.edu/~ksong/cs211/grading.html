Homework/Project Guidelines

  • Keep your code as neat as possible. Make sure to utilize proper whitespace and follow naming conventions. Points will be deducted for messy, illegible code. Provide sufficient documentation for full credit.
  • Although you are allowed to discuss these assignments with your fellow classmates, do not take code from your classmates, the internet, a tutor, and/or pay someone to write your code for you. If there is sufficient evidence of a violation of the CUNY Academic Integrity Policy, the student(s) involved will be given a 0 for the assignment and a report will be filed to the Dean and the Chair of the department. To prevent such an event, you are advised to start on your assignments as soon as possible and ask plenty of questions far before the due date.
  • Submit your assigments to: ksong.qc @ gmail.com (without the spaces) with the Subject line:
    CS211: LastName, Firstname - Homework# Submission
